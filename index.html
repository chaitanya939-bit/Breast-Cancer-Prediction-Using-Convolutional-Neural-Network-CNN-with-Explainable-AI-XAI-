About the System

Breast cancer is one of the leading causes of death among women worldwide. Early detection significantly improves survival rates and treatment outcomes. This project presents an AI-powered breast cancer prediction system that uses Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) to automatically analyze mammogram images and classify tumors as benign or malignant. The system also integrates Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI) techniques such as Grad-CAM to visualize important regions of the image that influenced the model's decision. This enhances transparency, interpretability, and trust for healthcare professionals.
